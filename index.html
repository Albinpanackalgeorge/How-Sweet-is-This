Toblerone: The Iconic Chocolate

Maybe you just need Toblerone.

Toblerone is a Swiss chocolate bar known for its distinctive triangular shape and unique honey-almond nougat flavor.

Toblerone Variants

Explore the different flavors of Toblerone: classic milk chocolate, intense dark, and smooth white chocolate.

Interesting Facts

  • Invented in Switzerland in 1908 by Theodor Tobler.
  • The iconic triangular shape is inspired by the Swiss Alps.
  • Comes in various flavors including milk, dark, and white chocolate.
